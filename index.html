What is Hollow Knight: Silksong? The Complete Guide

Hollow Knight: Silksong is a 2D action-adventure Metroidvania game developed by Team Cherry, released in 2025 as the official sequel to the critically acclaimed Hollow Knight (2017). This highly-anticipated sequel features Hornet, the princess-protector of Hallownest, as the playable protagonist in an all-new adventure through the mysterious kingdom of Pharloom.

Story Continues

Captured and taken to the unfamiliar land of Pharloom, Hornet must navigate this dangerous new world, battle its inhabitants, and uncover its secrets. Unlike the previous game's descent into darkness, Silksong follows an upward journey as Hornet ascends through the kingdom's citadel to reach the shining peak.

A Complete Standalone Experience

Silksong is a full sequel, not DLC or expansion. While knowledge of the original Hollow Knight enhances the experience, newcomers can jump right in and enjoy the complete story. The game features entirely new areas, enemies, bosses, mechanics, and a sprawling interconnected world that rivals or exceeds the original in scope.

Boss Battle Strategies

Conquer Pharloom's 40+ legendary bosses with these essential tactics

üéØ The 7-Step Boss Formula

  1. Phase 1: Observation Only ‚Äî Spend the first 30-60 seconds purely watching. Learn telegraphs, attack patterns, and recovery windows without greed.
  2. Hit Once or Twice, Then Move ‚Äî Most bosses allow 1-2 safe hits. The third strike usually invites punishment. "Hit and quit, don't commit."
  3. Silk for Survival, Not Show ‚Äî New players waste Silk on fancy moves. Pros save it for healing. You can't DPS if you're dead.
  4. Heal During Boss Recovery ‚Äî Only Bind when the boss is in a long animation, switching phases, or across the arena.
  5. Close Range > Far Range ‚Äî Silksong bosses telegraph more attacks at distance. Staying close often makes patterns more readable.
  6. Final Phase Caution ‚Äî Bosses below 10% HP are most dangerous. Play safer at the end, not more aggressive.
  7. One Lesson Per Death ‚Äî Don't try to fix everything at once. Identify your single biggest mistake and focus on that next attempt.

Hollow Knight: Silksong FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

üìÖ When was Hollow Knight: Silksong released?

The global release date was September 4, 2025, across PC, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4/5, and Xbox One/Series X|S platforms.

No, Silksong is a complete standalone experience. However, playing the original enhances your appreciation of characters and lore references. Recommended but not required.

Main story: approximately 15-30 hours. Full exploration and 100% completion can take 50+ hours. Your playtime will vary based on skill level and completion goals.

üïπÔ∏è Is there multiplayer?

No, the game is a purely single-player experience focused on narrative, exploration, and personal skill development.

üì± Is there a mobile version?

There is no official mobile version. Be cautious of any sites claiming to offer mobile downloads‚Äîthese are likely scams or unofficial ports.

üéØ What's the difficulty like?

üÜï Will there be DLC or updates?
