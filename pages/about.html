About This Site

Learn about our mission, editorial principles, and commitment to providing accurate Silksong guides

Our Mission

This website aims to provide practical, accurate and easy-to-follow guides for Hollow Knight: Silksong players. We focus on actionable strategies, verified mechanics and hands-on tips instead of hearsay.

Whether you're a newcomer to the Pharloom Kingdom or a seasoned Hollow Knight veteran, our goal is to help you master Hornet's abilities, conquer challenging bosses, and discover all the secrets Silksong has to offer.

Editorial Principles

  • ✓ Accuracy first: Information is tested in-game or cross-checked with official sources.
  • ✓ Clarity and usability: Step-by-step instructions, terminology explained, mobile-friendly layout.
  • ✓ Transparency: Sources are cited where appropriate and updates are timestamped.

About the Author

Maintained by a long-time Hollow Knight player and community contributor. Having completed the original game multiple times (including all Pantheon endings), I'm excited to document Hornet's journey through Pharloom.

I document routes, boss patterns and equipment synergies with reproducible methods. Every strategy shared here has been tested personally or verified by trusted community members.

Update Policy

Pages are reviewed regularly after game updates. As Silksong continues to evolve with patches and balance changes, I strive to keep all guides current and accurate.

If you spot inaccuracies, have suggestions for improvements, or would like to contribute, please reach out via the Contact page.

Note: This site is continuously updated as new information becomes available about Silksong's mechanics, boss strategies, and hidden secrets.
